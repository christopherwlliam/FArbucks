About Us

History & Innovation

History
FArbucks journey begins in December 2021, where the first coffee shop opened in Jakarta, offering some different coffees from around the world for our customers. Due to the exciting events and great taste of coffee, we have progressed rapidly and become one of the most popular coffee shops around the world.

Innovation
We introduce new, industry-leading and more efficient coffee brewing equipment. We also ensure quality consistency for FArbucks. Our coffee is made using only the highest quality ingredients sourced from around the world.
